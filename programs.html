Our Course

An Overview into our Foundations of Web App Development course

The current course consists of 8-weeks involving both fundamentals in architecture as well as hands-on programming along with demo skills and presentations. Students who complete this course are expected to be able to work in the area of product management and to be able to rotate to different roles within a team as long as the product is not too technical.

Regarding facilities, this course will be taught in Harbour Centre at SFU downtown. Students are expected to come on time and have all previous readings to be completed. Students are also required to bring their own laptops as the instructor wants to adopt a bring your own laptop (BYOL) policy. This is also done to provide students with a real glimpse of what industry professionals do.

Course Syllabus

Week 1 - The Computing Mindset
Determining goals and setting ground rules, with a brief introduction of the Ruby-on-Rails application.
Week 2 - Understanding Web Apps - The Rise of Apps
Introduction to Platform for vendor management and also introduces 'if' statements.
Week 3 - Agile Development, Project Design, and UI Flow
The introduction of a 'compiler' and the importance of this tool in development.
Week 4 - Databases, Databases, and Databases
Teaches Database structure, design, and brief introduction on optimization.
Week 5 - Testing, System Validation and Verifications (SV&V)
Topic on quality assurance, what industry does and the likes.
Week 6 - The power of "if" statements in development
Student demos followed by lesson on vendor management features.
Week 7 - Database Optimization
Techniques on database considerations and schemas
Week 8 - The Final Challenge!
Final presentation and evaluation along with a guest speaker from Beedie.
