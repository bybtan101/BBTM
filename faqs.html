Top questions from our students

How technical is this course?
That's a great question! This course is designed for entry-level non-computer science students so our instructor will teach you from scratch. However, we do expect hard work and our instructor will cover quite a bit during the 8 weeks
What methods of payments do you guys accept?
Upon registering, we do require a $50 dollar deposit, which can be paid for in our registration page. In the first week of class, we would need the remaining payments by either cheque or cash. We will provide a full receipt upon the clearing of the cheques.
Where is this course taught at?
This course will be taught within SFU's Segal School of Business in the heart of downtown. The actual instructions will be provided in an email upon registering
Who is the instructor? What is his profile?
The current instructor is Daruvin Sood, he has a BSC in computer engineering and physics at UBC. He also worked for huge corporations such as Microsoft, TELUS, and the Federal Government. He is currently a solutions architect at TELUS. He also has over 4 years of mentoring and teaching high-school and university students.
How is this different than other training courses/bootcamps?
That's an excellent question! Other bootcamps tends to be much more technical, focuses on purely the design, develop, and deployment of applications and systems. However, our course focuses on the fundamentals of working on IT projects in various roles. Essentially, we want our students to be able to pur on many hats in the IT space so providing knowledge in the areas of database management, developing, testing, and project management.
Are you affliated and owned by SFU?
Currently, we are not owned by SFU however, we are undergoing the final phases of securing a potential partnership with them.
