About Us

A story of three recent graduates

Acceltech was founded by three recent computer science and business programs individuals. All three of us aree passionate both in technology and in education so naturally we wanted to share our knowledge and experience with the rest of the student body. Due to the Greater Vancouver job market, we recognize that most entry level positions in the technology industry requires some sort of technical skills, they want well-rounded individuals that are knowledgable both in the business fields as well as the technology side.

Based on the current market needs and our passion about finding things to do on the weekend, we decided to start this 8-week program that is dedicated in providing high-school and university level students with the technical skills to not only create applications but also to understand what industry is looking for.
